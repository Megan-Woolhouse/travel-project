WHY NEW YORK CITY?

NYC, the city that never sleeps

There is something magical about New York City. From the hustle and bustle of Times Square to the iconic skyline, NYC is a city like no other. Whether you’re visiting for the first time or the hundredth time, it’s guaranteed to be an unforgettable experience.

Many people don't know or have forgotten that New York City, formerly named New Amsterdam, was the country's first official capital. As such, New York has sites of amazing historical value. Or if you're not into history, this modern city has a plethora of activities to fill your time. From broadway shows to bougie cafe's and bars, from stunning architechture to shopping till you drop, NYC has something for everyone.

CAFES

My favourite cafes in New York City

Angelina Bakery

A hidden gem planted in the hustle of Midtown, Baker Antonio “Tony” Park, brings the first Asian-inspired Italian bakery to New York City, serving exceptional baked goods transformed from recipes passed down by the matriarch of the family. These timeless classics are brought to life in a transparent laboratory for guests to admire in the production process.

Address:

575 8th Ave Between 38th & 39th Street, New York City, NY 10018-3011

What I like about it

Gorgeous Breakfast and Presentation. The look and taste of all the food was fantastic. The staff were also super friendly! Definitely recommended the filled croissants!

Broadway Bagel

It all Starts with a Bagel and a Schmear! Broadway Bagel only uses the freshest and highest quality ingredients in all their menu items. From their Signature sandwiches to their incredible pastries, you, your family and guests will always come back for more!

Address:

2658 Broadway, New York, NY, 10025

What I like about it

Broadway Bagels is not just a bagel shop. It is a full Deli and a place to get fantastic sandwiches and breakfast. They have a great selection as Bagels, cream cheese, deli meats, Cheese's and other items. There is no need to go anywhere else for your meal. You'll get everything you need right there.

Slate Cafe

Slate Cafe is a bustling restaurant and coffee bar with locations in NoMad and Midtown in NYC. The menu presents an abundant assortment of gourmet breakfast options and flavor-packed sandwiches, salads and small plates to enjoy in house or take away. They cater to nearly every food preference with a variety of gluten-free, vegetarian and vegan offerings.

Address:

121 Madison Avenue 30th Street, New York City, NY 10016

What I like about it

This adorable little spot is perfect for a coffee meet up; a midday writing, emails, and snack break; or a full on healthy lunch munch. I had a wonderful quinoa bowl with so much freshness and a thirst-quenching brown tea. I’ll definitely be back!
