WHY NEW YORK CITY?

NYC, the city that never sleeps

There is something magical about New York City. From the hustle and bustle of Times Square to the iconic skyline, NYC is a city like no other. Whether you’re visiting for the first time or the hundredth time, it’s guaranteed to be an unforgettable experience.

Many people don't know or have forgotten that New York City, formerly named New Amsterdam, was the country's first official capital. As such, New York has sites of amazing historical value. Or if you're not into history, this modern city has a plethora of activities to fill your time. From broadway shows to bougie cafe's and bars, from stunning architechture to shopping till you drop, NYC has something for everyone.
